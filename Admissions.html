Admissions

Join Heritage International College and embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal growth.

Admission Requirements

  • Passport Photograph.
  • Completed application form.
  • Academic transcripts or report cards from previous schools.
  • Letters of recommendation (if applicable).
  • Birth certificate or proof of age.
  • Health records and immunization documentation.
  • Payment of application and enrollment fees.

Admission Process

  • Submit your application online.
  • Provide required documents and information.
  • Schedule and attend an entrance examination.
  • Complete any additional assessments or interviews as required.
  • Receive your admission decision and next steps.
  • Complete enrollment paperwork and pay deposit fee (₦10,000).
  • Attend orientation and begin your academic journey at Heritage International College.
