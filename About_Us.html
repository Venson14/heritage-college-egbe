About Us

Heritage International College is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a culture of excellence. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in their chosen fields and make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of students.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning, preparing students for global challenges.

Our History

Founded in 1990, Heritage International College has a rich history of academic excellence and community engagement. Over the years, we have expanded our programs and facilities to meet the evolving needs of our students and the global workforce.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and ethical behavior.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in education and personal development.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and embracing new ideas and technologies.
  • Inclusivity: Fostering a diverse and welcoming community for all students and staff.
  • Community Engagement: Promoting social responsibility and active participation in community service.
